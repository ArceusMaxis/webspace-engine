Jel is a new way to meet with people and collaborate.

If you play video games, you'll feel right at home. 🕹


In Jel, others will see you as

a friendly avatar. When you talk,

your mouth will move!



To see how you look, look to the bottom left.

Unmute your mic, and watch yourself talk!

Click your avatar to change its color.

Moving Objects


Grab a duck by hovering and holding Tab

or your right mouse/trackpad button.


Drag to move. Scroll to push and pull.

Or just walk around while holding to place it.

Rotating Objects


Hover over objects and hold R to rotate.


Drag to rotate it on two axes.

Hold control or scroll to roll it on the third.
